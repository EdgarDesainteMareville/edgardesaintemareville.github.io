I am a first-year PhD student in the OCKHAM team at Inria, within the LIP laboratory at ENS Lyon, under the supervision of Elisa Riccietti, Paulo Gonçalves and Nelly Pustelnik.

I graduated in applied mathematics from Ecole Centrale de Lyon and the Maths en Action master’s program. I completed my master’s thesis at MaLGa, under the supervision of Matteo Santacesaria and Giovanni S. Alberti. More information can be found in my curriculum.

My research interests include multilevel optimization and learning-based methods for inverse problems.
